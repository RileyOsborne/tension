# Friction Design System

## Colors & Backgrounds
- **Primary Background**: Radial gradient `from-slate-900 via-slate-950 to-black`.
- **Card Backgrounds**: `bg-slate-900/40` or `bg-slate-900/60` with `backdrop-blur-xl`.
- **Borders**: `border-white/5` or `border-slate-800`.
- **Accent Colors**: 
  - Blue: `#2563eb` (Primary Action)
  - Green: `#16a34a` (Success/Start)
  - Red: `#dc2626` (Friction/Danger)
  - Purple: `#9333ea` (Presentation)

## Components
- **Heading**: `font-black uppercase tracking-tighter`.
- **Subheadings**: `font-bold uppercase tracking-[0.2em] text-slate-500 text-xs`.
- **Cards**: `rounded-[2rem]` or `rounded-[3rem]`, border, shadow.
- **Inputs**: `bg-slate-950/50`, `border-2 border-slate-800`, `rounded-2xl`, `px-6 py-4`. High contrast text.
- **Buttons**:
  - Tactile: `bg-{color}-600 border-b-8 border-{color}-800 rounded-2xl active:translate-y-1 active:border-b-0`.
  - Ghost: `bg-slate-800/50 border border-slate-700 rounded-2xl`.

## Layout
- Avoid "boxy" sidebars. Use large, floating containers with significant negative space.
- Use horizontal arrangements for player lists and round progress where possible.
